Song breakdown: Midsummer Train Station

by Umcaruje.

This song started out as a weird idea. I wanted to sidechain some chords that have a kind of retro-ish feel to them. So I hopped on and used SID to make them :)

What happened next is that I made the drums. I spent most of the time EQing and compressing them. As for samples, I used this lovely Musicradar sample pack which has some really nice drums.
